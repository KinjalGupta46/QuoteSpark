# QuoteSpark

**QuoteSpark** is a modern and interactive quote generator web application built with **HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript**. It empowers users to explore, generate, and personalize inspirational quotes through two intuitive modes — **Random Generator** and **Advanced Generator**.

---

## Features

- **Smart Random Generator**  
  Instantly explore a wide variety of quotes from curated categories like *Motivational*, *Tech*, *Nature*, *Humor*, and more — with just one click.

- **Advanced Quote Customization**  
  Generate personalized quotes by choosing your preferred **topic**, **tone**, and **length**, offering a tailored experience for every mood.

- **Dark Mode Support**  
  Switch effortlessly between light and dark themes for improved readability and a better user experience across all devices.

- **Voice Playback (Text-to-Speech)**  
  Let your quotes speak for themselves with built-in voice narration powered by the **Web Speech API**.

- **Favorites Manager**  
  Save and organize your favorite quotes locally using **localStorage**, so they’re always just a click away.

- **Downloadable Quotes**  
  Download your chosen quotes as `.txt` files for offline use or personal collection.

- **One-Click Copy**  
  Instantly copy any quote to your clipboard and share it across social media, notes, or anywhere else you like.

---

##  Technologies Used

- **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (Vanilla)
- **Icons & Fonts:** Font Awesome, Google Fonts
- **Browser APIs:** Web Speech API, localStorage
